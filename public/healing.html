HEALINGPROCESS

Please read carefully. The first 2-4 weeks of healing your tattoo or piercing is critical to how it will hold up for years to come!

If your artist placed a “second skin” product (SANIDERM/HYPAFIX) after your tattoo, follow your artist’s specifications for that product. If removed early, start instructions below depending on the day of removal.

TATTOO AFTERCARE

FIRST THINGS FIRST

The artist applies a thin layer of antibiotic ointment and a bandage. Keep this dressing on for just a few hours (no more than 24 hours). This protects from bacteria and irritation.

GENTLY WASH

Remove the covering after a few hours. Wash your hands first with fragrance-free antibacterial soap (Dove/Dial). Gently wash the tattoo with warm water and soap. Pat dry with a soft cloth or paper towel. Do not wipe.

MOISTURIZING

  • Days 1-3: For larger tattoos, apply the thinnest layer of Aquaphor 1-2 times daily. There should be no residue left. Too much suffocates the tattoo.
  • Days 3+: Switch to fragrance-free, alcohol-free lotion (Aveeno, Lubriderm, or Cocoa Butter).
  • NO: Vaseline, Coconut Oil, Triple Antibiotic Ointment, or Hustle Butter.

AVOID WHILE HEALING

  • Direct sunlight (and do not use sunblock on a healing tattoo).
  • Scratching or picking at scabs.
  • Tight clothing over the area.
  • Submerging in pools, hot tubs, or the ocean.

PIERCING AFTERCARE

DAILY CLEANING

All piercings should be cleaned at least 3 times daily with Dove/Dial antibacterial fragrance-free soap and warm water.

NO: Hydrogen Peroxide, Tea Tree Oil, Alcohol, or "Wound Wash." Only use antibacterial soap and water.

JEWELRY SPECS

All piercings at Electric Tiger are performed with surgical steel medical grade implant material (316L ASTM F-138), which is hypoallergenic and safest for most bodies.

WHEN CAN I CHANGE THE JEWELRY?

  • Earlobes: 4 to 8 weeks
  • Septum / Labret: 6 to 8 weeks (or longer)
  • Nostril: 3 to 4 months (or longer)
  • Cartilage / Tragus / Rook / Conch / Flat / Helix: 3 to 9 months (or longer)
  • Navel / Nipples: 6 to 9 months (or longer)
